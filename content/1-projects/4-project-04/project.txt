Working closely with the the teams at Mitchell & Ness and the NBL (National Basketball Association) to announce their uniform and apparel partnership for the the 16/17 season. Allowing the players to be themselves and showcase their personalities and skills. I wanted to take them out of their indoor/professional environments and back to the schoolyards and street courts they grew up playing on.
